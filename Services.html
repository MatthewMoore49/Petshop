ADOPTION/MICROCHIPPING

Microchipping is a permanent animal identification system that takes just seconds to administer. The CCSPCA highly recommends microchipping your pets in addition to collars and tags. The cost of a microchip, which includes registration done for you, is $25.00. Pets must be at least 8 weeks old to receive a microchip.

We are proud to partner with our local county SPCA for your adoption and microchipping needs.

*Microchip vouchers are readily available upon request*.
